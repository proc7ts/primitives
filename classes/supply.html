Class Supply

Supply handle.

Represents a supply of something.

The supply can be cut off, and can inform on cutting off.

off

  • off(reason?: unknown): Supply
    • Defined in proc7ts/primitives/src/supply/supply.ts:61

    Cuts off this supply.

    After this method call nothing would be supplied any more.

    Calling this method for the second time has no effect.

    Parameters

    • Optional reason: unknown

      An optional reason why the supply is cut off. It will be reported to whenOff callbacks. By convenience, an absent reason means the supply is done successfully.

    Returns Supply
